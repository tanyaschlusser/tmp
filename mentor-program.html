Mentor/Mentee posts

ChiPy's mentor program pairs experienced members of the Python community with an appropriate mentee match. The 10 week program has three tracks: Python 101, Web development, and Data Science, and at the end of the program, mentees present their projects at one of our monthly ChiPy meetups.
